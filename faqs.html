• What is CARTER?

    Carter is an e-commerce platform that allows you to order groceries and vegetables from local vendors, as well as sell scrap items. We aim to provide convenience and support local businesses and communities.

  • How do I register on Carter?

    You can register on Carter by clicking on the Profile icon on our homepage. You'll need to provide your basic details, such as name, email, phone number, and address. After registering, you'll receive a confirmation email or SMS with a link to verify your account.

  • Is Carter available in my area?

    Carter is expanding its services to various regions. You can check if we serve your area by entering your PIN code or location details on our website.

  • How do I place an order for groceries or vegetables?

    Browse through our list of available vendors and products. Select the items you wish to purchase, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Choose your preferred payment method and provide your delivery address. Your order will be confirmed once payment is successful.

  • Can I choose a specific vendor for my order?

    Yes, you can choose a specific vendor based on their location, ratings, and the products they offer. You can view the sellers and select the vendor that best suits your needs.

  • How can I sell scrap items on Carter?

    Simply browse through the various scrap items listed on our platform. You can view their buyer and place an order for your scrap. A scrap dealer will then contact you and arrange to collect your scrap from your location.

  • How do I get paid for my scrap items?

    Payments for scrap items will be arranged directly between you and the scrap dealer at the time of collection. Ensure you agree on the payment terms before finalizing the collection.

  • How can I contact customer support?

    You can reach our customer support team through the "Contact Us" page on our website. We offer support via email, phone, and live chat.

  • What is your refund policy?

    We offer refunds for orders in cases where the product is damaged, incorrect, or if there are issues with the delivery. Please refer to our refund policy for specific conditions and timeframes.

  • How can I request a refund?

    To request a refund, go to the "My Orders" section of your account, select the order you wish to return or refund, and follow the instructions. You can also contact our customer support team for assistance with your refund request.

  • How long does it take to process a refund?

    Refund processing times vary depending on the payment method used. Typically, refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after approval. You will receive a notification once the refund has been completed.
